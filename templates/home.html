Insider Watchlist

This webapp is designed to scrape SEC data and capture instances where multiple high level officers purchase stock from their company on the same day.

Is Insider Trading Legal?

Insider trading involves using non-public information for stock trades. It's illegal if it's based on material non-public information, but legal if company insiders report their trades to the SEC. This publicly accessible information promotes market transparency. Non-insiders can gain insights from these trades, assisting their investment decisions and promoting a fair marketplace.
